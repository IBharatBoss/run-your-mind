The Dopamine Reset: How to Reclaim Your Brain from Reels

Your attention span is dying a slow death. It's time to take it back.

Ever opened Instagram for "just 5 minutes" and realized it's 2 AM? Bhai, you aren't alone.

Your brain is literally being fried by 15-second loops of nonsense. Asliyat yeh hai ki tum lazy nahi ho, bas tumhara reward system hack ho chuka hai.

1. The Reality Check

Reels are designed by engineers to keep you hooked. Every swipe gives you a tiny hit of dopamine.

Eventually, real life starts feeling boring. Reading a book? Faltu. Working on a skill? Too slow.

You have a 3-second attention span now. If it's not fast, your brain wants to swipe. This is a trap.

2. The "RYM" Strategy

We don't do "motivation porn" here. We do systems. You need to reset your baseline.

Dopamine fasting isn't about sitting in a cave. It's about making low-stimulation activities rewarding again.

Stop reaching for your phone the second you wake up. That first hour decides if you're a leader or a follower today.

3. The 10-Minute Action Plan

Reset Protocol

Step 1: Move the App. Take Instagram off your home screen and hide it in a deep folder.

Step 2: Grayscale Mode. Turn your phone screen to black and white. Colors trigger dopamine; grey is boring.

Step 3: The 20-Minute Rule. No scrolling until you've done 20 minutes of "boring" work (reading/coding).

The world wants you distracted so it can sell you stuff. Stay sharp, execute hard.
