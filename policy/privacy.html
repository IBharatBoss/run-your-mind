Privacy Policy

How we respect and rigorously protect your personal data.

We value your privacy as much as we value your time. Our data collection is extremely minimal and focused entirely on providing you a smooth, uninterrupted learning experience.

1. What We Collect

When you sign in using Google Authentication, we securely receive your Name and Email Address. During the checkout process, our payment gateway (Razorpay) may request your phone number solely to process the transaction securely and send you a receipt.

2. How We Use It

Your email is strictly used as your unique identifier to create your premium profile and ensure you have permanent access to the library. We do not use your email to spam you with useless newsletters or promotional noise.

3. No Data Selling. Ever.

Your data belongs to you. We guarantee that we do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third-party marketing agencies. All your profile data is heavily encrypted and secured using Google Firebase infrastructure.
