Cancellation & Refund Policy

Clear, honest, and straightforward rules regarding your purchase.

At Run Your Mind, we believe in keeping things completely transparent. When you purchase the lifetime access to our platform, you instantly unlock our entire premium digital library and all future updates.

1. Strict No-Refund Policy

Because our product is 100% digital and provides you with immediate, unhindered access to curated knowledge, we do not offer any refunds or cancellations. Once a payment is successful and access is granted, the sale is considered final.

2. Payment Failures

If your money is deducted but your premium access is not activated (which happens rarely due to network drops), please don't worry. The amount will either be automatically refunded by your bank within 5 to 7 working days, or you can contact our support team to manually activate your account immediately.
